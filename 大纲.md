|UnaryOperator<T>     | T->T               |  iterate
|BinaryOperator<T>    | (T,T)->T           |   reduce
|BiPredicate<L,R>     | (L,R)->boolean     |
|BiConsumer<T,U>      | (T,U)->void        |    collect
|BiFunction<T,U,R>    | (T,U)->R           |    reduce
